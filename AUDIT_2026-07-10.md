**Correction logged:** a first test pass reported RLS insert failures on every form.

## 🔴 Launch blockers

### 1. Self-signup is blocked (email confirm ON, no email provider)
Tested: `auth.signUp()` returns **no session** → Supabase requires email confirmation,
but B8 (email) isn't built, so **no confirmation email is ever sent**. A real visitor
signing up is stuck. Admin-created users work (they bypass confirm).
**Fix:** either disable "Confirm email" in Supabase Auth (soft launch) OR ship B8 email.

### 2. Payments do not exist
No Stripe code, no PayPal, no checkout/webhook anywhere. `STRIPE_*` env values are
placeholders. Memberships are display-only (`/membership` links to signup, not payment);
conference/dinner collect interest, take no money. Also: **Stripe can't onboard Albanian
businesses** — a provider decision (Paddle + Payoneer, or Raiffeisen RaiAccept) is needed
before B4 can be built.
